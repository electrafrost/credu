Network School is located on Forest City, a modern island development in Johor, Malaysia — forty minutes from Singapore's Changi Airport. Cost of living is significantly less than Singapore, Australia or any major Western city.

The advantage here is continuity of attention.

Serviced accommodation, meals, gym training and shared working spaces are all onsite. No commuting. No cooking. No domestic logistics. Remove the daily tasks that fragment your cognitive bandwidth. When you're not constantly switching contexts between life's usual demands, you can actually make progress.

You'll work within a global community of innovators and technologists while remaining active in your own practice or remote role. Singapore is across the strait for conferences and business engagements.

🏫 Network School

A global community of remote workers, founders, professionals and technologists. Membership covers accommodation, coworking, high-speed wifi, gym with personal trainers, three healthy daily meals, and access to all programming — talks, workshops and events. It's a serviced lifestyle.

🎤 High-calibre presenters

Network School runs a full and active calendar of sessions led by experienced technologists and founders from diverse fields. Daily sessions cover applying AI tools, learning by building, content creation, and taking products to market. The calendar changes weekly, but the format stays consistent: active, participant-driven learning.

🏠 Apartment living

CREDU Academy participants share serviced apartments — with full living spaces, and room to work, study and live for three months. Suitable for those with a partner or children. This is a working residency, not a hotel stay.

🌏 Strategic for Asia-Pacific

Easy access from Australia, India, China and across Southeast Asia — with a comfortable time zone for working remotely. Singapore next door for flights and the broader tech and business ecosystem. Network School offers Malaysian visa support for longer stays.

The CREDU programme

Learn Hands-On & Implement

This is a working residency, not a curriculum. There are no spectators, no tracks, no levels.

Three months of structured immersion produces more applied output than a much longer period of fragmented evenings and online study. CREDU adds accounting industry focus to Network School's regular programming and is customised around what each cohort brings and where they need to get to.
