Problems

Addressing Challenges in Meeting Professional Requirements

The accounting profession aims to bolster public trust and elevate perceptions of its value. Yet, many accountants struggle or neglect to maintain their relevant continuous education requirements. We investigated why.

💸

Expenses and Hassles

CPE/CPD programs can be costly. Maintaining detailed records with certificates from different sources is tedious, with no automations.

📚

Specialization Gap

Content is often basic or generalist, and can be lacking specialization for diverse professional levels, niches, or growth needs, especially if the product is low-cost or free.

🔍

Not Being Audited Enough

Verifying members' CPE/CPD hours is a time-consuming and costly task for professional bodies. It's not feasible to do at a large scale or in real-time.

👁️

Accountability Incentive Gap

Consumers and regulators have no visibility into accountants' current educational achievements, a key to building trust and attracting clientele...and motivating compliance.

Credu Solution

Simplified Open Credentialing With Incentives

We are simplifying the task of CPE/CPD record keeping for accountants, at no cost to them, with verifiable and descriptive records on a transparent and easily auditable platform... which also unlocks new revenue streams. A net profit approach!

Simplified Credential Management

Automatically issues CPD/CPE certificates with details to your public Credu profile, updating logs of hours and competencies, aggregating all credentials for real-time compliance tracking.

Accessible and Free

Offers a globally accessible platform that is free to use for professional accountants to simplify their CPE/CPD tracking and publicly promote their expertise.

Audit Efficiency Breakthrough

Eliminates auditing time and costs for the accounting bodies by automating CPE/CPD compliance monitoring and verification across the membership base. Overseeing members are meeting standards is managed through a single dashboard.

Transparent, Monetised Expertise

Empowers accountants and trainers to co-create specialized CPD content, monetizing expertise in a marketplace, while offering visibility to consumers seeking specialized knowledge.

Roadmap

Credu Development Journey and Core Foundations

2024 – 2026

Research, Validation, Proof of Concept, Litepaper

Plan & develop pilot project: prototype for accountants & CPE providers.

Q3 2026

MVP 1: Build and Test

Build and test with accountants and CPE providers.

Q1 2027

MVP 2: Expand Development

Expand development to enable functionality for all users.

Q2 2027

Pilot MVP With All Users

Pilot MVP with all users including accounting bodies and P2P-CPD marketplace.

Version 1 of the CREDU app will be built leveraging existing applications to showcase its concepts and potential. We will conduct a pilot with a test group of digital accountants and CPE/CPD training providers. Our MVP 1 is to ensure validation and inspire industry ambassadors to pitch the app to our professional accounting bodies.

Development on MVP 2 will then be expanded to enable all four user groups to interact: accountants, CPE training providers, professional bodies, and the public, with more advanced tooling to validate the platform's efficacy.

CREDU is to be developed as a PUBLIC GOOD using open-source software, without proprietary systems, shareholders or equity investors. Users will be rewarded based on their contributions. This approach is to ensure that value and decision-making are distributed among those who actively contribute to the platform's growth and success.

Why Public Blockchain?

The Importance of Open-Source Foundations for a Truly Global Profession in the Accounting Industry

Transparency & Trust

Open-source code fosters trust by allowing public inspection to verify it does what it claims to do.

Community Ownership

Ensures all stakeholders can contribute and influence the project's direction for collective benefit.

Collaboration & Innovation

Global contributions drive continuous improvement, increasing the project's value to more users.

Independence from Central Control

Remains free from control by any entity or accounting body, to ensure impartiality.

Security & Reliability

Peer-monitored for quicker identification and fixing of issues by the community, enhancing security.

Cost Efficiency

Leverages community contributions and game theory principles to reduce development and maintenance costs.

Adaptability

Easily customized for a global community to meet diverse professional needs and regulatory requirements.

Sustainability

Not reliant on a single group or the cooperation of professional bodies; community driven for long-term relevance.

About Us

Who's Behind Credu

Electra Frost

Chartered Tax Adviser & Accountant

electrafrost.com ↗

CREDU, and its associated DAO, CREDAO, is a social innovation project under the auspice of Digital Playhouse Foundation Ltd, a purpose-built Australian charity and Public Benevolent Institution, advancing public welfare through adaptation of existing skills to emerging technologies.

The project is led by Digital Playhouse co-founder and CFO, Electra Frost, a Chartered Tax Adviser and Accountant in Australian international professional practice since 2008.

As a business technologist with a long-term view, she is based at Network School in Malaysia/Singapore since September 2024. She is contributing to building the NS startup society and campus model with a Minimum Viable Community (MVC) that will develop education content and scale the CREDAO community and CREDU platform & dApp.

Building the Team

Electra is currently assembling a transdisciplinary team of educators, advisers and builders from emerging tech, finance and accounting fields to guide the project.

The CREDU platform is being developed through CREDU Academy, a rolling cohort programme hosted as a Network School residency in Malaysia/Singapore — building the platform and its community from the ground up with each intake of practitioners.
